Building a new home for Henshaw Fox

Henshaw Fox are a team of like-minded experts who promise to take the stress out of moving house. With an entirely people-focused attitude and expertise in the local areas, their ability to deliver a consistently high level of service, regardless of size, is unmatched.

Website

We worked closely with Henshaw Fox to rebrand the business, focusing entirely on their values and ethos as a selling point. We produced a brand new website, featuring bespoke copy that spoke specifically to Henshaw audiences. This website included extras such as lifestyle videos that showcase the local area of the property and features such as detailed filtering of properties and tools to hover show property interior.
